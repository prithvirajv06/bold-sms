School Administrator

Teacher

Access to classroom management, grading system, and student communication.

Key Permissions:

  • Attendance Tracking
  • Grade Entry
  • Parent Communication

Department Head

Extended teacher access with departmental oversight capabilities.

Key Permissions:

  • Teacher Management
  • Curriculum Development
  • Department Reporting
  • Budget Access (View)
  • Schedule Management

Student

Limited access to personal records, assignments, and communication tools.

Key Permissions:

  • View Assignments
  • Submit Work
  • View Grades
  • Communication Tools
  • Calendar Access
